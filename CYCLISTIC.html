SUMMARY OF ANALYSIS

It was interesting working with this data and analyzing it, i will state the insights i got from this analysis: The total number of rides in the year 2022 is 4,369,052, Annual Members - 2,611,005 and casual riders - 1,758,047

Three bikes were used by the riders: Classic, Electrical and Docked bikes. The most used bike by both riders is the classic bike. Only the Casual riders used the Docked Bikes.

The busiest time of the day for the riders is in the afternoon, with 17:00hrs being the most rushed hour for both riders.

For the days in the week, the riders rode differently. The Annual members rode more during the week with Thursday being their busiest day- and Sunday being the least day. The casual riders used the bikes more on weekends with Saturday being the busiest day and Tuesday, with the smallest number of rides.

The busiest month of the year for Annual Member is August - 335,201 with July - 330,980 closely behind. For the casual Riders, July was the busiest month in year 2022 with 311,649 number of rides. The least rides in a month for both riders was the month of January, with February being the 2nd least. Summer being the busiest season and Winter the least.

The total average ride length of all riders is 17 mins, the average ride length by Annual member riders is 12 mins and for casual riders is 23 mins

Streeter Dr & Grand Ave is the most popular start station used by causal riders by 55,054 rides and Kingsbury St & Kinzie St is the most popular start station among the Annual member riders by 23,523 rides.

ACT

My final conclusion is that the riders rode a bit differently in bike type and settings. Getting the casual riders to become members is very possible because they are frequent riders and they could save their resources by becoming members.

BUSINESS RECOMMENDATIONS

  1. According to the insight gained from this data, it shows that casual members ride the most on weekends with afternoon being the busiest time of day, so I would recommend some kind of business advert or marketing on our membership subscriptions introduced to the casual members. The locations that should have major concentration should be at their most used start stations which is Streeter Dr & Grand Ave and the timing should be on weekends.

  2. The classic bikes and docked bikes are the most used by the casual members, we can include a 10% discount in the subscription packages for the first three months on Docked bikes since only casual members use it, and on the classic bikes. Also, we can purchase more docked bikes in the first 10 most visited start stations where casual members use docked bikes.

  3. Summer is the busiest period in the year for casual members, especially in the months June and July. I would suggest a large scale digital marketing with the use of social media platforms like instagram, twitter, facebook and snapchat. These apps run business ads and promotions. So having a strong online prescence is important. Also, collaborations with influencers, like gym inflencers (cycling is a good form of exercise), celebrities, sport wear companies e.t.c on these social media platforms can boost digital marketing. Billboard adverts during summer is also a marketing strategy for awareness.
